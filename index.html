About

Hi, my name is Yunhao Li. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence at Guangzhou University. My research interests focus on Computer Vision, Medical Image Analysis and Multimodal Learning. I have strong hands-on skills, proficient in tools like PyTorch, Transformers, AutoGluon, and server-side technologies including Vue, FastAPI, and Spring Cloud. My achievements include multiple national competition awards and patents. Ultimately, I aim to help others reach their full their potential through education.
